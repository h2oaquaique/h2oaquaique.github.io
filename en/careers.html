H2O offers lifeguard positions throughout the year.  
Mainly in the greater Montreal area, but also in the Quebec City and Sherbrooke area.
 H2O also offers positions for technicians and maintenance workers for swimming pools and spas.

  Our biggest hiring period is in the spring to fill the many outdoor pool lifeguard positions. Our training program is designed to allow our employees to feel confident to start their new job.
